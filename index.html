ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Yasya, and I’ve been a makeup artist for 3 years. Everyday I feel so fortunate to have a career in a field I am passionate about. While makeup is my medium, I’m mostly excited about people. While I'm a full-time makeup artist I also do a lot of work with social media, branding, business, and promoting local wedding vendors in the commercials.

Makeup and skincare is a huge passion of mine. I believe in the value of investing in others and I strive to make every interaction with my clients, colleagues, family and friends one of positivity and growth. My goal with every client, no matter the occasion, is that they feel relaxed and beautiful inside and out. When they step out of my makeup chair I want them to feel inspired and supported. I'm so thankful for anyone who has ever hired me, worked with me, supported me, or sipped a coffee with me.
